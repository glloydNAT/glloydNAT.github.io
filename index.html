Projects

I am currently working on a number of software projects relating, in some way or other, to my archaeological work. One such project is:

  • OCACal: A cythonized revision of iosaCal which also extends the functionality to allow for automated input of radiocarbon dates stored in a variety of formats and schemas in order to populate a database. Like iosaCal, it can also provide charting and non-database outputs. OCACal is not publicly available at this time, but will be made available pending its completion.
